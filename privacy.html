Privacy Policy

Quiktricity LLC

Quiktricity Controls is a service name used by Quiktricity LLC for system notification, email, and SMS alert communications.

Effective Date: March 2026

Overview

Quiktricity LLC ("Quiktricity," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected in connection with industrial automation and PLC system SMS alert services operated under the Quiktricity Controls notification name.

Information We Collect

  • Mobile phone number
  • System alert configuration and preference data

How Information Is Used

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver transactional SMS messages related to PLC system alerts, equipment malfunctions, and operational status notifications. Messages are sent only when triggered by system events.

SMS Messaging

By opting in, users consent to receive automated, transactional SMS messages related to their equipment.

  • Message frequency varies based on system activity
  • Message & data rates may apply
  • Reply STOP to opt out
  • Reply HELP for help

Information Sharing

Quiktricity LLC does not sell, rent, or share personal information for marketing purposes. Information is shared only with service providers required to deliver SMS messages and only for that purpose.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
